FAQ

  frequently asked questions:

What are your hours?   ACS is open 8:00 am to 6:00 pm everyday except Sunday but if it’s an emergency,
give us a call and we’ll work something out.

What are payment methods?   ACS accepts all credit and debit cards, cash and even bitcoin!

Do I pay the tech directly?   You can if you would prefer or you can be billed and pay online.

Should I tip the tech?   No tip is necessary but if you feel like thanking us for doing a great job,
give us a positive review on Google or Yelp!

How can I book an appointment?   Book appointments HERE! or call 702.706.2848.

How can I track the status of my repair?   You will receive constant updates via text or email as to
the ongoing status of your repair and there will never be any
additional charges without your approval.

Can my computer be fixed remotely?   Yes, ACS can affect repairs remotely using free software called
Anydesk. https://anydesk.com/en. You will download small a portable program that does not install
into your system and can be easily deleted. You will then give the technician the code given by
Anydesk and grant permission to access your desktop. You will then see the tech on your screen
making repairs. Once done, you can delete Anydesk and your system will no longer be accessible.
