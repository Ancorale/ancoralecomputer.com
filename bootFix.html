so your pc won't boot up...

  cuz I ain't greedy and it's super simple:


  1. Get you a flash drive with at least 8 gigabytes.
  2. You will need a copy of Windows. You can get one HERE! (use the Media Creation Tool)
  3. Follow the instructions ↑↑↑
  4. You are going to need to enable your computer to boot using the flash drive.
    Typically, you can get into the boot menu by into by tapping one of the funtion keys, usually F8.
    If that doesn't work, google it.
  5. When your computer reboots and says 'Hit any key', do it.
  6. Click the NEXT button in the bottom right when you see it.
  7. On the bottom right of the next page, you'll see 'Repair your computer'.
    Click it.
  8. Choose Troubleshoot.
  9. Advanced options.
  10. Command Prompt.
  11. Type 'D:' and ENTER in the command prompt
  12. Type 'bootrec.exe /fixmbr' and ENTER.
  13. type 'bootrec.exe /fixboot' and press ENTER.
  14. If you don't get 'The operation completed successfully' after each,
    try 'bootrec.exe /rebuildbcd' and press ENTER.
  15. If that don't work, call me. 702.706.2848
